About NCC Special Entry Scheme

The National Cadet Corps is the Indian military cadet corps with its headquarters in New Delhi. It is open to school and college students on a voluntary basis. The NCC Special Entry Scheme is one way to honor former NCC cadets' achievements in society and thank them for their significant time.

A direct selection procedure known as SSB enables NCC cadets to enlist as an officer in the Indian Armed Forces. The NCC Special Entry Scheme training offers young people in our nation plenty of chances to develop their personalities and moral character. Numerous camps, including the RD parade, provide sufficient exposure to and training in physical fitness, weapon training, and adventurous activities. The NCC Special Entry has significantly contributed to secularism, unity of the country, and selfless service values over the years.

Eligibility Criteria

  • The minimum age for the NCC and army battle ward candidates is 19 years, and the maximum age is 25 years
  • Degree from an accredited university or an institution equivalent, with a minimum cumulative grade point average of 50%
  • NCC Special Entry Physical Standards: (a) Height and Weight - The minimum acceptable height is 157.5 cms with co-related weight.

Selection Process

Candidate selection for NCC Special Entry 2022 will take place in two stages. Shortlisting of Candidates, SSB Interview, and Medical Examination will be used to choose the candidates.
