Join The Indian Army As Technical Officer With TES Scheme

About TES Scheme

The Technical Entry Scheme (TES) is a recruitment scheme for the Indian Army. It is designed to select candidates who have completed their 10+2 with Science subjects and are interested in joining the Army as Technical Officers.

The TES scheme is a direct entry for young and dynamic candidates who want to serve the nation as Technical Officers in the Indian Army. The selected candidates are trained in various aspects of military tactics and leadership skills.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Candidates must have completed their 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics (PCM) with a minimum aggregate of 70% and above.
  • The minimum age limit is 16.5 years, and the maximum age limit is 19.5 years.
  • Candidates should be unmarried male and of Indian nationality.

Selection Process

The selection process for TES Scheme consists of two stages:

  1. Shortlisting of candidates based on their 10+2 marks and SSB interview call letter.
  2. SSB interview, which is conducted in two stages and includes various tests to assess the candidate's aptitude, intelligence, and personality traits.
